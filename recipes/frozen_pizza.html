Frozen Pizza!

Description

This recipe is another fan favorite. Even though frozen pizzas are normally knows as depression in a bite, this one actually has real flavor and gets a nice crunch to it.

Ingredients

  • Wegmans Thin Crust Frozen Pizza
  • An oven

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven
  2. Cook pizza
  3. Take pizza out of oven after it is nice and crispy
  4. Cut pizza into 1/4s
  5. Let pizza cool
  6. Burn mouth
  7. Let pizza cool more
  8. Enjoy
