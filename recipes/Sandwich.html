Easy to Make Sandwich

Description

A basic sandwich that I make for lunch when I have back to back meetings. No judging.

Ingredients

  • Wheat Bread
  • Deli Meat of Your Choice (I get Buffalo Chicken)
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • SPICY BROWN MUSTARD

Steps

  1. Put Two Halfs of Bread Down Separately
  2. Put Deli Meat on Sandwich
  3. Put Swiss Cheese on Sandwich
  4. Put SPICY BROWN on Sandwich
  5. Eat Sandwich
  6. Realize You Forgot to put Lettuce on Sandwich
  7. ...you really have to eat more vegetables
  8. I SAID NO JUDGING
