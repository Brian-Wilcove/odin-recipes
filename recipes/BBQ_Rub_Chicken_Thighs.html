BBQ Rub Chicken Thighs

Description

This is an easy recipe that is guaranteed to be a fan favorite.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Dry Rub of your choice
  • Not an ingredient but Air Fryers make this recipe POP

Steps

  1. Take Chicken out of package
  2. Put dry rub on chicken
  3. Put MORE dry rub on chicken
  4. Put Chicken in Air Fryer at 400 degrees for 10 min
  5. Flip chicken and cook at 400 degrees for another 10 min
  6. Let rest for 5 minutes then dig in!
